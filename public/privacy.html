Privacy Policy

Solo Forge · Last updated 6 July 2026

Solo Forge is a local-first Android fitness app. It is designed so that your data stays on your device. The developer does not collect, receive, store, or have access to any of your data.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no backend, no account system, no analytics, and no crash reporting. The developer cannot see your meals, weight, fasting history, workouts, settings, or any other information.

Where your data lives

All data you enter — meals, weight entries, fasting history, workouts, settings, and exports — is stored locally on your device. Android's cloud backup is disabled for the app, so this data is not copied to Google's servers. You can create a full backup to a single file, and optionally auto-backup to a folder you choose. Those files stay wherever you put them.

The one network feature

Solo Forge makes a network request only when you start food photo analysis. In that case, the photo or text you submit is sent to OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) using an API key that you provide. This feature is opt-in: it does nothing until you enter your own key, and it can be disabled at any time. Data sent to OpenRouter is governed by OpenRouter's privacy policy. No other part of the app sends anything over the network.

This website

This website is static. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and does not track visitors.

Children

Solo Forge does not collect data from anyone, including children.
